Palak paneer

back to main cource home

ingredients

  • 500 grm panner,250 grm palak
  • 1 medium onion
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • red dry chili,bay leaves,cumin seeds
  • fresh cream
  • kasturi methi

instructions

  1. add oil heat the oil.
  2. then add dry chilli,bay leave,cumin seeds
  3. add chopped onion and ginger-garlic paste,stir well for 5 min.
  4. boil the palak(spinach) and cool it down in ice water .
  5. make the paste of palak.
  6. then add this paste in the pan,stir for 15 min.
  7. add panner lead the pan .
  8. add fresh cream and then kasturi metthi.

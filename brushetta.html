  • tomato,garlic

instructions

  1. prepare tomato topping chop properly
  2. prepare the bread and heat the grill pan grill it for 2-4 min
  3. rub garlic over the bread.
  4. finish it with topping and add mozerella
  5. add few drop of olive oil,salt,pepper and basil.

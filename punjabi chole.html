ingredients

  • 2 cup cutted tomato
  • 1 cup boiled white chana
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • chole masala

instructions

  1. add oil in pan than add cumin seed.(stir well)
  2. add chopped onion and stir well
  3. add cutted tomato,stir well.
  4. add chole masala and water
  5. add the boilled chana.
  6. lead the pan and serve it.

choco-cake

back to desserts home

ingredients

  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp coco-powder
  • 2 eggs
  • luke warm milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350 degree f(175 c)
  2. mix flour,coco-powder and sugar in a bowl.
  3. add eggs and vanilla ectract,stir well.
  4. add milk ,stir well
  5. bake for 20-25 minutes.
